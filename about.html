Our Vision

At Surasara radio, our vision is to be a valued, vital and vibrant service to our listeners . One that influences them to view the world from various perspectives, and utilize emerging opportunities effectively. This will positively serve our audience and engage our communities.

Our Mission

We aim to apprise, inspire and entertain all our listeners through a multitude of programs organized in both Sinhala and English, while strengthening their civic and cultural lives.
